# Rock Paper Scissors

A fun and sleek digital version of the classic Rock Paper Scissors game. Play against the computer and see who comes out on top!

## What is this?

This is a simple hand game where you pick either Rock, Paper, or Scissors and compete against the computer. It follows the age-old rules:

- **Rock** beats Scissors
- **Scissors** beats Paper
- **Paper** beats Rock

If both you and the computer pick the same thing, it's a tie!

## How to Play

1. Open the game in your web browser (just double-click the `index.html` file)
2. Click on one of the three hand buttons at the bottom: Rock, Paper, or Scissors
3. Watch the hands animate and see who wins the round
4. Your score and the computer's score update automatically
5. Keep playing as many rounds as you like!

### Keyboard Shortcuts

You can also use your keyboard to play faster:

- Press **R** or **1** to pick Rock
- Press **P** or **2** to pick Paper
- Press **S** or **3** to pick Scissors

## What You'll See

- **Scoreboard at the top** - Shows your score vs the computer's score
- **Hand animations in the middle** - Realistic hand illustrations show your pick and the computer's pick
- **Three buttons at the bottom** - Click these to make your choice
- **Game stats** - Shows total rounds played, your wins, losses, and draws
- **Recent rounds history** - A list of all your recent plays so you can see your track record

## Features

- Dark theme with a clean black, grey, and white design
- Realistic hand illustrations that shake during the reveal
- Smooth animations throughout
- Works on computers, tablets, and phones
- Keyboard support for quick gameplay
- Score tracking and game history

## How to Run

### Option 1: Just open the file

1. Download or save all three files (`index.html`, `style.css`, `script.js`) into the same folder
2. Double-click on `index.html`
3. The game opens in your web browser

### Option 2: Using a local server (for developers)

If you have a local web server (like XAMPP, WAMP, or VS Code's Live Server):

1. Place the files in your server's website folder
2. Open your browser and go to the local address
3. Play the game!

- The computer picks randomly, so there's no way to predict its choice

- Check the Recent Rounds section to see your latest plays
